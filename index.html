Your Inner Compass

Discover Your True North
A Personal Values Exercise by Victoria Eliseeva

This exercise helps you identify the values that act as your inner compass — the principles that guide your decisions, relationships, work, and life. You will start with 100 values and narrow them down through a fast, visual tile-based sorting process.

Step 1Clear the Noise

Remove the values that are clearly not important for you. They fade out like discarded tiles.

Step 2Find What Matters

From what remains, select up to 30 values that feel meaningful.

Step 3Choose Your Anchors

From your shortlist, choose the final 5 values that feel most essential.

Step

Clear the Noise

How to play

Treat this like a values Mahjong board. Move quickly. Click what is clearly not you. Use the small “?” on a tile to read its definition.
